About the Event

The Gibs Juneteenth Cornhole Tournament is a competitive 2-person team cornhole bracket held every June 19th to celebrate Juneteenth, the start of summer, and the joy of spending time with great people. Come ready to throw bags, cheer loudly, and make memories that last a lifetime!

Free-Play Activities

In addition to the cornhole bracket, enjoy these games at your own pace throughout the day:

  • 🍺 Beer Pong
  • 🥤 Flip Cup
  • 🤝 Team Chug
  • 🎉 And more!
